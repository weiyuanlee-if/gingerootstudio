Everlasting Horizon

Everlasting Horizon is interactive fiction game about being transported into an isekai. The universe of Everlasting Horizon was built by a bored engineer wishing to escape his repettive life, it then becomes viral on the internet causing players to flood his own world. A game about self-discovery and the perils of escapism.

Shopholic

A young woman is granted infinite money to finally shop at one of the biggest malls in town. However, Panda Block Mall has garnered some controversy as it's being expanded into a sacred land housing spirits. A choice/life simulator game about overdevelopment and shopping addiction.

Tower of Solitude

A young boy visits his grandmother in a run-down part of Chicago. He then finds an abandoned city, where a group of spirits hold stories that most cannot tell or find anywhere else.
